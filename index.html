Avanti's Pizzeria

Medina, N.Y.

2015-2018

Avanti's was my first job in high school. Even though I had worked on and off for my family's farm for years,
this was my first taste of real employment. I learned the in's and out's of restaurant service work and an
introduction to the joys of the customer service industry. Little did I know this job wold lay the groundwork for
my current career. My time there ended when I started college, though i still visited when I was home from school.
